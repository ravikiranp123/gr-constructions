About our Company's founder

G. Ranganathan

Founder of GR Constructions Mr. Ranganathan has been in the field of construction since 1989. After much hard work, he started GR Constructions in 2004.

GR Constructions has completed multiple types of civil engineering projects such as residential, commercial, industrial factory buildings as well as ifrastructure projects such as masonry, concrete and earthen dams.

About our Company

We've been creating

With a strong focus on maintaining Safety, Economy, excellent Quality, and delivering within the promised Time frame, GR Constructions has gained much accolades from clients and fellow industrialists alike.

We maintain mixer machines, vibrators, steel shuttering for about 20000 sft as well as VDF Equipment. The ability to quickly procure on demand helps us get started as soon as the contracts are finalised.

We now provide Architectural, Structural and Service design consultancy as well to augment and maintain our high standards.
